# arena-fps-godot

A wave-based first-person survival game set on a floating platform in an unknown space. Fight off increasingly large waves of hostile vultures while avoiding being pushed off the platform's edges.

<a href="https://pthrrr.github.io/arena-fps-godot/" target="_blank">Play in browser</a>

![Single Player](screenshots/singleplayer.png)

## Story

You wake up standing on a platform, suspended in a vast, empty void. There is nothing else -- no ground, no sky, no horizon. Just the platform beneath your feet and silence all around.

At first it seems harmless. Still. Almost peaceful.

Then you hear it. A low, mechanical hum, faint at first, growing louder. You look around and see them: strange machines, hovering in slow orbits around the platform. They pulse with a dull light. You do not know what they are. You do not know what they want.

Then the machines activate. One by one, they begin releasing creatures -- bat-like things with sharp, angular wings. Vultures. They swarm toward you. Their movements are focused. Aggressive.

Your hands tighten around a weapon you did not notice you were holding. You fire. Each shot connects. But they keep coming, and they are not trying to hurt you -- they are trying to push you. Their bodies slam into yours, shoving you backward, toward the edges, toward the fall. You dig in. You hold your ground. You survive.

The attack subsides. But the calm does not last. The machines activate again. More creatures this time. Each assault brings more enemies, the pressure harder to resist. You fight wave after wave, until eventually the force is too much and you are pushed over the edge.

You fall.

## Features

- Keyboard + mouse controls
- Wave-based survival gameplay
- Vultures that chase and knockback the player
- Orbiting spawners with escalating spawn rates
- 45-second round timer
- Score tracking, kill plane
- Custom sky shader with gradient and fog
- Menu and gameplay music with looping
- Sound effects (shooting, hit, kill)
- Smoke puff VFX on mob spawn/death

## Branches
